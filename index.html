Welcome to the Campus Carbon Footprint Calculator

Our goal is to help students and staff understand their carbon footprint on campus and provide simple, practical tips to reduce it.

With CarbonTracker, you can easily calculate your monthly electricity use, weekly transport distance, and paper consumption, then receive personalized advice.

Together, we can create a greener campus and contribute to a healthier planet.

Navigate through the sections above to start calculating, learn helpful tips, meet the founders, or share your valuable feedback.

Tips to Reduce Carbon Usage

  1. Turn off lights and devices when not in use.
  2. Walk or bike instead of using motor vehicles on campus.
  3. Participate in campus recycling and tree-planting drives.
  4. Use reusable bottles, bags, and utensils.
  5. Minimize Food Waste.
  6. Regularly service your car and keep tires properly inflated.
  7. Turn off lights and unplug appliances when not in use.
